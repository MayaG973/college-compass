Overview

NJIT is a public research university located in Newark, New Jersey. Established in 1881, it has a rich history of providing high-quality education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as doctoral degrees. With a focus on innovation and entrepreneurship, NJIT is known for its strong partnerships with industry and its commitment to advancing technological research and development.
