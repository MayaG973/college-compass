Fairleigh Dickinson University (FDU)

Overview

Fairleigh Dickinson University (FDU) is a private university with campuses in the metropolitan area of New Jersey and New York City. Founded in 1942, FDU offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs designed to prepare students for success in their careers and communities. The university is committed to providing a transformative educational experience characterized by academic excellence, global engagement, and personal growth.
