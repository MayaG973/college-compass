Ramapo College of New Jersey

Overview

Ramapo College of New Jersey is a public liberal arts college located in Mahwah, New Jersey. Established in 1969, Ramapo offers a distinctive undergraduate education characterized by small class sizes, interdisciplinary studies, and experiential learning opportunities. The college is known for its scenic campus, strong sense of community, and commitment to academic excellence, social responsibility, and sustainability.
