Rutgers University - Camden

Overview

Rutgers University - Camden is a public research university located in Camden, New Jersey. Established in 1926, it is one of three regional campuses of Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. Rutgers-Camden offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs in various fields, including the arts and sciences, business, law, nursing, and public affairs. With a commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and community engagement, Rutgers-Camden provides students with opportunities for intellectual growth, personal development, and social impact.
