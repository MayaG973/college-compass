Drew University

Overview

Drew University is a private liberal arts university located in Madison, New Jersey. Established in 1867, Drew is known for its strong emphasis on the liberal arts, personalized education, and close-knit community. The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs and is recognized for its beautiful wooded campus, often referred to as "The Forest."
