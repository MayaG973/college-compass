Stockton University

Overview

Stockton University, founded in 1969, is a public university located in Galloway, New Jersey. Known for its scenic campus near the Jersey Shore, Stockton offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs. The university is dedicated to providing a high-quality education, fostering a diverse and inclusive community, and promoting academic excellence and civic engagement.
