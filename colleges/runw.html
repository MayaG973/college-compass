Rutgers University - Newark

Overview

Rutgers University - Newark is one of the three regional campuses of Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. Located in Newark, New Jersey, it offers a dynamic urban setting and is known for its diverse and inclusive community. Rutgers-Newark provides a wide array of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines, including arts and sciences, business, criminal justice, law, nursing, and public affairs. The campus is dedicated to research excellence, social justice, and community engagement, preparing students to become leaders and innovators in their fields.
