Rutgers University - New Brunswick

Overview

Rutgers University - New Brunswick, often referred to simply as Rutgers, is the flagship campus of the Rutgers University system. Founded in 1766, it is one of the oldest higher education institutions in the United States. Located in New Brunswick, New Jersey, Rutgers offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs across various disciplines. With a diverse student body and a strong commitment to research and community engagement, Rutgers is recognized as a leading public research university in the nation.
