New Jersey City University (NJCU)

Overview

New Jersey City University (NJCU) is a public university located in Jersey City, New Jersey. Established in 1927, NJCU is known for its diverse student body and commitment to providing affordable and accessible education. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs and is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement.
