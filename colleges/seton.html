Seton Hall University

Overview

Seton Hall University is a private Roman Catholic university located in South Orange, New Jersey. Founded in 1856, it is one of the oldest diocesan universities in the United States. Seton Hall offers a broad range of undergraduate and graduate programs and is renowned for its strong emphasis on academic excellence, ethical development, and service to the community. The university is home to a diverse and inclusive student body and provides a nurturing environment that fosters personal growth and professional success.
