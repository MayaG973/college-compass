Monmouth University

Overview

Monmouth University is a private university located in West Long Branch, New Jersey. Established in 1933, Monmouth offers a comprehensive array of undergraduate and graduate programs, emphasizing academic excellence, experiential learning, and professional preparation. The university is known for its picturesque campus, strong sense of community, and commitment to student success.
