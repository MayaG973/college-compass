Stevens Institute of Technology

Overview

Stevens Institute of Technology is a private research university located in Hoboken, New Jersey. Established in 1870, Stevens has a rich history of innovation and entrepreneurship. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, business, and the humanities. Known for its rigorous academic curriculum, experiential learning opportunities, and strong ties to industry, Stevens prepares students to become leaders in their fields.
