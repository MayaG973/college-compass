Kean University

Overview

Kean University is a comprehensive, public university located in Union, New Jersey. It is known for its strong focus on teaching, commitment to research, and community engagement. Established in 1855, Kean offers a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs and has a reputation for providing quality education at an affordable cost.
