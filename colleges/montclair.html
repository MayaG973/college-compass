Montclair State University

Overview

Montclair State University is a public research university located in Montclair, New Jersey. Established in 1908, Montclair State has evolved into a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. With a focus on academic excellence, diversity, and community engagement, Montclair State prepares students to become well-rounded professionals and leaders in their fields.
